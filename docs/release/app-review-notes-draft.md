# App Review Notes — Orangutany

For Apple App Review submission.

---

**App Name**: Orangutany

**Description**: Mushroom identification app using AI. Users take or upload photos of mushrooms and receive AI-powered species identification including edibility information, look-alike species, and morphological traits. The app includes educational content about mushroom species.

**Account Types**:
- Free users: 5 daily scans, can browse educational content, save identifications to their account.
- Pro users: Existing web subscribers who sign in see their Pro membership status. There are no in-app purchases — Pro subscriptions are managed on the web.

**Native Features**: Camera capture, photo gallery picker.

**Test Steps**:
1. Launch the app.
2. Tap the camera or gallery icon to add a mushroom photo.
3. Tap "Analyze" to submit the photo for identification.
4. View identification results (species name, edibility, traits, look-alikes).
5. Sign in to save identification results to your account.
6. Browse educational content via "Learn About Mushrooms" in the navigation.
